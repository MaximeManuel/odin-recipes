Pour-Over Coffee

The most stylish cup of coffee


  1. Grind 1 cup of coarsely ground coffee beans.

  2. In a large jar, add the ground coffee. Slowly pour 4 cups of water over the beans as you stir until the coffee is thoroughly saturated.

  3. over the container with a lid or plastic wrap. Place it in the refrigerator and let it steep for at least 12 hours.
  • You can brew up to 24 hours, depending on your personal taste.
