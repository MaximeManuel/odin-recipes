Cold Brew Coffee

Iced coffee for warm days


This simple brewing method produces a richer, sweeter flavor with the true taste of coffee beans. It's almost as concentrated as espresso, but without the bitter taste or high acidity of hot-brewed coffee.

Freshly ground coffee beans and cold or room temperature water are the only ingredients needed.


Steps

  1. Grind 1 cup of coarsely ground coffee beans.

  2. In a large jar, add the ground coffee. Slowly pour 4 cups of water over the beans as you stir until the coffee is thoroughly saturated.

  3. over the container with a lid or plastic wrap. Place it in the refrigerator and let it steep for at least 12 hours.

  4. Filter the beans from the coffee. Pour slowly and stop as needed to avoid overflowing the filter.

  5. Serve and enjoy ❤️

Tips

  • You can brew up to 24 hours, depending on your personal taste.
